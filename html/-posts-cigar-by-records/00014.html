Cigar Log: Phillies - Blunt Strawberry 3 (Top Half)

Previously:
• Cigar Log: Phillies - Blunt Strawberry 3 (Bottom Half)
• I Bought More Phillies Cigars... and my not-so-great plan

This is the top half of my 3rd Phillies Strawberry cigar pack. It has sweet flavours at the first few draws. For some reasons, this half was quite spicy that I felt the spice part way down my lungs. I did not draw all the way down, but maybe I breathe it in through my own second hand smoke.

The ash actually grew out to be pretty long, I didn't quite capture this on camera 😅. It's also got quite good amount of smoke output so you will definitely catch people's eyes if you are walking down the street.

It was a short walk from buildings to buildings. The smoke lasted about 10-15 min at a low-wind night. The evening was cozy and it wouldn't be any better to call it a cigar night.

I got a sweet start of the smoke, but still didn't pick up any strawberry. I really did not like that sharp spice taste. Having that said, I did pick up some strawberry scent from my clothes after the smoke though.

I still have two more Phillies strawberry left. I think I have a good idea about what this cigar taste like, but I'll continue to try different variations of puffs to see if I can get anything different. That's all I have for you today. See you in the next post!
