Cigar Review: Phillies Blunt Cognac Cigar

Phillies Blunt cigarillos do not come with cigar bands since Canadian plain packaging law. Since I have the strawberry one still, I just use sticky tabs to identify them. Pink is strawberry, and orange marks the cognac. Today, we will be smoking the cognac flavour.

In turns of the flavours, the wrapper was extremely sweet. There were nothing complicated with the flavour from the smoke though. Similar story with strawberry cigarillos, I could not taste any cognac. Only the very artificial sweetness on my lips. Other than that, it was pretty much just smoke.

Phillies is not known for being smooth and I did taste a little bit of sharpness towards the mid section. It could be that I was smoking too hot and too fast that slightly overheated the cigar. I basically just let it sit for a while before I took another puff and everything was the same again.

The smoke time for this one was about 25min. It was not too windy and not too warm. The weather was rather calm. The burn line stayed fairly consistent till the end. I fully enjoyed my time and the casual walk with this cigarillo.
