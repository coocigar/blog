Cigar Log: Phillies - Blunt Strawberry 3 (Bottom Half)

This is the third stick of my Phillies cigar pack. As usual, I cut this one in half and today we will be taking a look at the bottom half the cigar.

The wrapper of this stick did not fall off completely, but it was slightly detached on this end. I pretty much just held on to it with my hand so it does not come loose until I finished the cigar.

I had this cigar near the airport. It was extremely windy at the time of this session. The smoke time for this half was only a mere 5 minutes. The ash stayed pretty long until it finally got blown off by the strong wind.

I didn't taste anything sweet, strawberry or sharpness for this one. It was just light tobacco and it pretty much just went out after a few puffs. Having that said, I did smell some strawberry sweet flavour from the hand that I was holding the cigar. 🍓

I should have probably just smoke the full one instead of cutting it in half. Anyways, I still have two and a half sticks left. Hopefully I can get the flavour in the next few tries.
